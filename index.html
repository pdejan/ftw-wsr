ABOUT

Canidae [FTW] gets involved in a variety of WvW game play (with a focus on havoc/ BL's), in a mature, supportive and socially comfortable, fun environment. The approach of the guild is one of co-operation and inclusiveness. Guild members are involved members who take pride in participation, and whose input is welcomed and valued.This guild aims to be an off-peak presence in Piken. A place to congregate and chill. It is not a HC fight guild, but members are free to cross-rep for that with other guilds.

We are always looking for:

  • We are looking for people who enjoy a range of WvW activities.
  • We want players from off-peak times: Mid-day and Overnighters especially welcome.
  • We want guildies who are willing to commit to having a nice place to hang out, with friendly people.
  • We want guildies who are team players, attend our guild events, and like to do stuff with others.
  • Players of all skill levels welcome, however it is expected you will be running a server or guild build and have a commitment to better play.
  • 100% rep rule, when you are doing things with the guild.
  • TS3 essential.
  • 18+ mature-minded people with manners
